Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I ever use this website?

Pocatello Eats is designed to help people who are just itching to try something new in Pocatello, whether they're longtime residents or just visiting for a weekend.

Can I limit the options I see on the map?

Of course! All you need to do is select what filters you'd like to use from the 'Filters' dropdown menu.

I can't find xxxx restaurant!

Oh no! Please send us message giving us the details so we can add it to the list.

How do I save my preferences?

It's actually really easy to save your preferences with us. All you need to do is create a free account and your favorite restaurants and filters can be saved for easy access the next time you want to find it again.
